Israel's Cabinet says it will not ratify Wye River accord until Palestinians crack down on terrorism.
 Yasser Arafat must convene Palestinian National Council to abrogate Palestinian National Charter, which calls for Israel's destruction.
 Israeli Cabinet also announces it will begin to build houses in controversial Jewish settlement.
 Decision comes less than three hours after car rigged with explosives blew up in an outdoor market in downtown Jersualem.
 Two people were killed and 21 others were wounded in the attack for which the Islamic militant group Hamas claimed responsibility.
