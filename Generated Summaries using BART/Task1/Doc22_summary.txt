Qin Yongmin tried for the second time in a week to legally register a human rights monitoring group.
 Qin said a civil affairs official in the Hubei provincial capital of Wuhan accused him of engaging in illegal activities.
 Qin, detained briefly two weeks ago, was questioned for about three hours before being released.
 Wang Youcai, Qin Yongmin are accused of inciting the subversion of state power.
 They face from 10 years to life imprisonment if convicted under the harshest aspects of the law.
