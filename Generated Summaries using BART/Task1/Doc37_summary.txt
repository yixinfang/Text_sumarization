Ariel Sharon is being considered for the government's No.
 2 post.
 He would be a pivotal figure in reaching a final peace settlement with the Palestinians.
 Sharon is the general who led Israel's 1982 invasion of Lebanon.
 He fought in the 1948 Mideast war and was wounded three times.
 In 1953 he formed elite commando unit ''101'' and led it on raids against Palestinian guerrillas.
 In 1982 he was indirectly responsible for Lebanese Christian militiamen's massacre of Palestinian civilians.
