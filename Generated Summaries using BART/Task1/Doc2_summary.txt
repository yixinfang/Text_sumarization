Honduras on maximum alert as Hurricane Mitch churns through northwest Caribbean.
 President Carlos Flores Facusse declares state of maximum alert.
 Honduran military sends planes to pluck residents from their homes on islands near the coast.
 Up to 15 inches (38 centimeters) of rain forecast in mountain areas.
 Aid workers struggle to reach survivors of Hurricane Mitch.
 Officials estimate more than 10,000 people have been killed by the storm.
 Taiwan says it will donate dlrs 2.6 million in relief to Honduras, Nicaragua, El Salvador and Guatemala.
