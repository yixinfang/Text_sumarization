Fire broke out about midnight (2300 GMT Thursday) in building of local Macedonian Association.
 Crowd in second story of building contained mostly 13 to 18 year olds.
 Facility had approval for a maximum capacity of 150, detective says.
 Cause of fire has not been determined as of Friday evening.
 Fire is the deadliest in modern Swedish history; in 1978, 20 people died in hotel fire.
 Police say another 173 people were injured, 20 of them severely.
 The cause of the fire that broke out just before midnight Thursday remains under investigation.
